What is Brown Adipose Tissue (BAT)?
Unlike regular white fat that simply stores calories, brown adipose tissue is metabolically active. It contains a high concentration of mitochondria and is specifically designed to burn calories as heat. Think of it as your body's internal furnace. When BAT is working properly, it can incinerate hundreds of extra calories per day with zero effort on your part.

Researchers at Harvard Medical School have been studying BAT since the 1970s. What they found was extraordinary: lean people consistently have significantly higher levels of active BAT than overweight people. It wasn't correlation — it was cause and effect.
